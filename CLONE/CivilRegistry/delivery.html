Delivery Reminder

The Philippine Statistics Authority – Marikina City is committed to ensuring that your requested documents are delivered safely and on time. This section provides timely reminders and important information about the delivery of civil registry documents, PhilSys IDs, and other official materials.

Track Your Documents and Stay Informed

May 26, 2025 – PhilSys ID Delivery

If you completed your PhilSys Step 2 Registration on or before April 30, 2025, please expect your PhilSys ID to arrive via PHLPost within the next two weeks. Make sure someone is available to receive the package at your registered address.

May 24, 2025 – Birth and Marriage Certificates (Online Requests)

All online requests made between May 15–20, 2025 through PSA Serbilis or PSAHelpline.ph are now in transit. Standard delivery time is 3–5 working days within Metro Manila. For tracking, refer to the confirmation email sent to you upon payment.

Reminder
Please present a vallid ID when receiving your document.
For failed delivery attempts, contact the courier immediately or coordinate with PSA Marikina for re-delivery.
Make sure your name, address, and contact details were correctly input during your request.
