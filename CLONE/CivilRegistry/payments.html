Payment Method

We are aware of how valuable your time, convenience, and safety are to you. We offer multiple payment options to choose from in paying for your orders at eSertipiko.ph. eSertipiko.ph.

Choose the payment option that is most convenient for you as you process your online eSertipiko application for a birth, marriage, CENOMAR, death certificate, or CENODEATH. You can pay online using a credit card or mobile wallet apps; you can pay over the counter at one of our accredited payment centers; or you can pay through partner banks. Click below for the payment channel you prefer.

Online Payment

Pay for your certificate orders by way of the website through your GCash credit, and then you should wait until your certificates are to be delivered.

Pay for your orders by using your PayMaya wallet. You can use your PayMaya wallet. You are able to simply scan the QR code in order to get your certificates. When PayMaya is chosen as the payment channel, the code is generated.

eSertipiko accepts payments from Visa and Mastercard credit cards. Pay through the website after you place orders then wait for PSA certificates to be delivered.

For your eSertipiko orders, use Bayad Online to pay. Downloading the mobile app plus using the website allows direct payment.

eSertipiko now accepts payments through QRPh. As the secure QR codes are standard for the Philippines, pay for your certificate orders securely as well as with peace of mind.

Over the Counter

You are able to pay for each of your certificates at any 7-Eleven store through the use of the CLIQQ machine. Ensure that you bring your Reference Number when visiting their store.

Bayad offers methods for certificate payment. Two convenient ways are available now! Visit the nearest Bayad store so you can pay over the counter or download as well as load up then pay via the Bayad app.

Pay for your certificates at Palawan Pawnshop in your area. The nearest one could be quite convenient.
