Updates and News

Welcome to the official Updates and News section of the Philippine Statistics Authority – Marikina City. Stay informed about the latest statistical activities, data collection efforts, announcements, and public advisories relevant to residents, stakeholders, and partner institutions in Marikina.

Latest Announcements & Activities

May 26, 2025 – 2025 Census of Population and Community-Based Monitoring System (CBMS)

The PSA Marikina City Office will begin preparatory activities for the 2025 Census of Population and CBMS. Field enumerators will be deployed in selected barangays for training and mapping operations starting June 3, 2025. Your cooperation is encouraged to ensure accurate data collection for better planning and services

May 22, 2025 – Civil Registration Services Update

PhilSys Step 2 registration services will be temporarily suspended on May 29, 2025, for system maintenance. Regular operations will resume on May 30, 2025.

May 18, 2025 – Hiring Field Staff for the Upcoming Surveys

The PSA Marikina is currently hiring field personnel for upcoming statistical surveys. Interested applicants may submit their resumes at our office or via email: esertipiko.marikina@gmail.com. Deadline for applications is June 7, 2025.
