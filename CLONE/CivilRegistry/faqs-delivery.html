PSA Certificate Delivery

Can you deliver anywhere in the Philippines?

We have a nationwide delivery service but there are some remote areas that are outside of our delivery coverage.

Why can’t you deliver outside the Philippines?

Our delivery partnership is currently set for local distribution, but we are working on expanding our reach to areas outside the country.

How long will it take you to deliver my document?

The delivery of your document is based on your delivery address.

  • Metro Manila: Next day after PSA releases the document
  • Provinces: Within 3–8 working days

Is there a chance my PSA certificate delivery will be delayed?

Unfortunately, yes. There are exceptional cases when the certificate cannot be immediately fetched from the PSA's database and must go through manual verification by PSA retrievers. This may add about 7 extra days of processing.

Other delivery delays may occur in areas under hard lockdown. In such cases, we comply with government rules and hold delivery until the lockdown is lifted.

Delays can also occur when the requesting person is unavailable at the time of delivery.

What do I need to prepare for delivery?

For a smooth delivery and the security of your document/s, please prepare any valid ID from our list of acceptable IDs and present it to the courier upon delivery.

We suggest assigning an Authorized Person to Receive just in case you will not be available. This can be done by clicking "I want to assign an Authorized Person to Receive the document" at the last stage of placing your order. An email with a secure link will be sent to your registered e-mail address.

What are the valid IDs accepted for PSAHelpline.ph as a delivery requirement?

Please see the list of acceptable IDs below:

  • Philippine Identification Card
  • Philippine Identification System Digital ID (ePHILID)
  • Philippine Passport (DFA)
  • Driver’s License (LTO)
  • PRC ID
  • IBP ID
  • GSIS UMID/eCard
  • SSS UMID
  • Pag-IBIG Loyalty Card
  • COMELEC Voter’s ID
  • Postal ID (PhlPost)
  • Senior Citizen’s ID (OSCA or LGU)
  • OFW ID (DOLE)
  • OWWA ID
  • Seaman’s/Seawoman’s Book (MARINA)
  • Diplomat/Consular ID
  • NBI Clearance
  • PNP ID or Police Clearance
  • DSWD Certification/4Ps ID
  • Barangay ID with picture and signature
  • PWD ID (NCDA or authorized issuers)
  • Government Office IDs (AFP, DAR, DOH, etc.)
  • Local Government IDs (Governor, Mayor, etc.)
  • TIN Card with photo and signature
  • School/Student ID (18+ only, from DepEd or CHED-recognized schools)
  • Company ID (from BSP, SEC, or IC regulated entities)
  • For Foreign Nationals:
    • Passport issued by a foreign government
    • Alien Certification of Registration (ACR I-card)
    • Immigrant Certificate of Registration
    • Special Resident Retiree Visa (PRA/BI)
